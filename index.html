Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of spare parts does Anura Group supply?
Anura Group specializes in supplying a comprehensive range of OEM and aftermarket spare parts for Japanese and Indian vehicles, motorcycles, and related automotive applications. Our portfolio also includes batteries and lubricants, ensuring complete solutions for the automotive sector.
Are your products genuine OEM quality?
Yes. We supply products that meet OEM specifications and industry quality standards. Our sourcing process focuses on reliability, durability and compliance to ensure optimal performance under Sri Lankan road and operating conditions.
Do you distribute islandwide?
Yes. Anura Group operates through a strong and established islandwide dealer and distribution network, ensuring consistent product availability and timely supply across Sri Lanka.
Who are your typical customers?
We serve a broad customer base including automotive dealers, workshops, fleet operators, industrial clients, and retailers. Our solutions are tailored to meet both large-scale OEM requirements and aftermarket needs.
How does Anura Group ensure product quality?
Quality assurance is a core priority at Anura Group. We follow strict inspection, sourcing, and compliance procedures, maintain strong international supplier partnerships and continuously monitor product performance to ensure customer satisfaction and long-term reliability.
