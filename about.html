About Us

Who We Are

Anura Group is a professionally managed business organization with a strong foundation in Sri Lankan entrepreneurship. Since our establishment, we have focused on building sustainable businesses through quality-driven operations, ethical governance, and customer-centric thinking.

Our diversified portfolio reflects our ability to adapt, innovate, and scale while remaining firmly committed to local values and national development.

Mr.Anura Sarath Kumara

Founder Of Anura Group


A Foundation Built on Trust

Anura Group was established with the belief that strong values create strong businesses. From the beginning, our focus has been on quality, integrity, and perseverance—principles that continue to guide the group today.

As the organization evolves, this foundation remains unchanged, shaping our growth and reinforcing our responsibility to contribute positively to Sri Lanka’s business landscape.

- Founder -



Mr.Lahiru Mewan Kottage

Managing Director


Leading with Responsibility

At Anura Group, our leadership philosophy is grounded in discipline, trust, and long-term vision. We believe sustainable success is achieved by balancing performance with responsibility—towards our customers, employees, and the nation.

I am proud of our team’s commitment and grateful for the confidence placed in us by our partners. As we move forward, we remain focused on strengthening our capabilities, embracing innovation, and delivering consistent value.

- Managing Director -
